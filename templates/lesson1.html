Marathi Lesson 1: Marathi Basics

Alphabet

The Marathi alphabet has 52 letters, including 36 consonants and 16 vowels.

Consonant Pronunciation
क ka
ख kha
ग ga

Pronunciation

Marathi is a tonal language, which means that the meaning of a word can change depending on the tone of voice used.

Grammar

Marathi grammar is relatively simple, with a subject-verb-object word order.
